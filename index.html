The grant problem is not funding. It is visibility after funding.

Ecosystems fund builders, but often lack a reliable way to see who is executing, which milestones are slipping, and which projects are becoming measurable ecosystem value.

1

2

3

Impact is measured too late

By the time outcomes fail to appear on-chain, recovery is already harder.
