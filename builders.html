The builder problem.

Most builders do not fail because they lack ideas. They lose momentum because execution becomes unclear and progress becomes hard to see.

How MAP works.

The system is simple on purpose. Goals become milestones. Milestones become weekly commitments. Commitments become execution reputation.

01

Goal

Define what the project is trying to achieve.

02

Milestone

Break the goal into visible delivery checkpoints.

03

Commitments

Choose weekly actions that move the milestone forward.

04

Score

Measure consistency and execution quality.

05

Reputation

Build a track record of reliable delivery.

Accountability Engine.

MAP does not only track activity. It identifies execution risk early and pushes builders toward recovery.

Warning

Missed 1 week. Review workload and recommit.

At Risk

Missed 2 weeks. Focus on the highest-priority milestone.

Recovery Plan

Missed 3 weeks. Reduce workload and rebuild momentum.

Critical

Missed 4 weeks. Project progress may be stalled.
